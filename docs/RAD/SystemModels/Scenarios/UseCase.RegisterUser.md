##### UseCaseFactCheckArticle

***Participating Actors***

Initiated by Star.

***Entry Condition***

1. Star needs to check if a Wikipedia article "Far-right" has been tampered with.

***Flow of Events***

1. Star copies and pastes "Far-right" into the WikiSpy search bar and presses ButtonSearch.
  A. WikiSpy checks if the article has been tampered with and loads the ScreenArticle.
2. Star types in "foo baby".
  B. WikiSpy responds with "Ooooo yeah baby!"

***Exit condition***

1. ArticleWidget GUI output is closed.
